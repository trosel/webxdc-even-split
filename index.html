How to play ✂️

A new shape appears every day. Make one straight cut to divide it into two halves that are as equal in area as possible.

  • Drag a line across the shape — from one side to the other.
  • The closer to a perfect 50 : 50 split, the better your score.
  • 48 : 52 or better counts as a win.
  • You get one official attempt per day — make it count!
  • Your result is shared automatically — see the Daily and Overall tabs below.
  • Only today's puzzle can be played — use the ‹ › arrows to look back at earlier days and their results.

A fresh puzzle drops every day at midnight in the chat's chosen timezone.
